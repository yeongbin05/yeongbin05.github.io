## 풀이 코드

```python
t = int(input())
for _ in range(t):
    n = int(input())
    coins = list(map(int,input().split()))
    m = int(input())
    dp = [0 for _ in range(m+1)]
    dp[0] = 1
    for i in coins:
        for j in range(i,m+1):
            dp[j] += dp[j-i]
    print(dp[m])

    
```
